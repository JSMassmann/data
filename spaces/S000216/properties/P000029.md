One can easily construct infinite sets $\{A_\alpha: \alpha < \mathfrak{c}\}$ so that $A_\alpha \cap A_\beta$ is finite for $\alpha \neq \beta$. Then $\{[\emptyset, A_\alpha]: \alpha < \mathfrak{c}\}$ is an uncountable family of pairwise disjoint nonempty open sets.
