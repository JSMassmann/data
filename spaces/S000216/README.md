Let $X = [\omega]^\omega$, the set of infinite sets of natural numbers, and give it the basis consisting of $[s, A] = \{B \in X: s \subseteq B \subseteq A \cup s \land \max(s) < \min(B \setminus s)\}$ for finite and infinite, respectively, $s, A \subseteq \omega$.
